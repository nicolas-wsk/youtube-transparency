Our mission
Be a watch dog for the impact of algorithms.
Our beliefs
Algorithms could make the world a better place for everybody. Tiny flaws in them can affect millions. We need independent organizations to investigate the impact of algorithms on sociaty.
Questions we want to answer
  • • Which algorithms are aligned with your needs?
  • • Which algorithms can you influence?
  • • Which algorithms can be abused?
  • • Which algorithms have learned to break the law?
Monitoring Elections
Algorithms already have a huge impact on our elections, through recommendation, search results, etc... No algorithm can be "neutral", hence we need to know which type of content they promote.
